My work

I'm Jorn Jaap Prenger, Audio Engineer, Producer, Singer Songwriter and Composer.
My interests include but aren't limited to exploring ways of using instruments in nontraditional ways,
using electronic effects and acoustics of spaces to distort and deform sounds,
creating interdisciplinary artwork and collaborating with musicians and artists in other fields.
Here are some examples of my work.
Most is played by myself, except for the vocals and percussion on Revelation,
which were composed and played by Sam Wolters.
